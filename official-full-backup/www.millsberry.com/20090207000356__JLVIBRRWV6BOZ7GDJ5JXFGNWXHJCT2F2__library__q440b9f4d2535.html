Welcome to the Millsberry Academy Library! The library's towering shelves contain a wealth of knowledge from the greatest minds of both the past and present. Here, students enjoy not only the library's vast collection of encyclopedias, essays, and books on famous literature, but a wonderful fiction section, too. It's a very cozy and comfortable place, and students often gather at the large tables after school to cooperatively finish their homework—which leaves the rest of the afternoon free for fun!
