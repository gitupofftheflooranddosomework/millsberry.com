Millsberry Police Station
The Millsberry Police Station, located in the heart of downtown, is home to the local law enforcement. The Chief of Police is Arthur Crow: a tall, skinny friendly man who keeps the peace in our city. Because of his size and stature, he earned the nickname “Scarecrow” in school, and it stuck. Sheriff Crow is a likeable fellow who keeps an eye on the streets and makes sure the peaceful community of Millsberry stays that way.

If you would like to know more about keeping safe around Millsberry, be sure to check out the Internet Safety section.

Also, the Sheriff keeps a Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Q.) list available for anyone who gets a little lost.

And, to keep the lawyers happy, Sheriff Crow keeps a copy of our Terms and Conditions and our Privacy Policy handy, just in case anybody needs to read through them.

Finally, Sheriff Crow is always looking for new deputies. Take a look below, and you'll see why.
