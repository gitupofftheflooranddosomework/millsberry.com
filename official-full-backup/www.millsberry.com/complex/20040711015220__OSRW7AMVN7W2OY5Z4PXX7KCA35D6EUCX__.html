Entertainment Complex
This is where the action is! Meet your friends here, hang out at the snack bar and pick up some popcorn or a candy bar. When you’re done, you can drop by the theater and check out a movie or two. Then, if you’ve got some spare change, you can spin over to the arcade to play the latest and greatest video games!

The hottest games right now are Hop-N-Drop and Cinnamon Toast Crunch. One’s a tricky jumping game and the other has a lot more to do with luck than skill. Why not give ‘em a try?
