Sylvane Lake
Is that Sylvie?! No, it's just an old tire. Someone should fish that out of the lake.

Nestled in a mountain range just outside of Millsberry, Sylvane Lake makes the perfect getaway. In the summer, the lake is a vacationer's dream, where visitors can swim, water-ski, or simply sunbathe on the beach. The lake and its surrounding areas are open for fishing, boating, picnicking, and hiking during the rest of the year, too. Whether it's spring wildflowers or colorful autumn leaves, Sylvane Lake always has something beautiful to offer those who visit its shores.
