Millsberry Academy
Welcome to Millsberry Academy! This used to be the site of the old school house, but as the city grew, the school house needed to grow, too. The Millsberry Historical Society didn’t want to see the building destroyed, so the City Hall officials agreed to build the Academy a little to the East. Now, the old school house looks down on the Academy from its high hill.

The Academy has a swimming pool, tennis court, baseball diamond and football field. It also has a vast number of less athletic clubs! The library is a treasure trove of knowledge, but beware of Mrs. Fiddle, the librarian. If you’re late with a book, she’ll come looking for it!

Only Summer Session is in progress now, and the only class is the spelling bee team practicing with Mrs. Seashore. They're playing a game called "Solver." It is tricky, but lots of fun! When school's back in regular session there will be a lot more things to do. Until then, just be glad you don't have to sit through Mr. Grumpalot's math class when it's so nice outside!
